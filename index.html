Configure Outlook Manually

To configure your email accounts manually with Microsoft Outlook, follow these steps:

Step 1: Open Microsoft Outlook on your computer. Click File > Account Settings > Manage Profiles.
Step 2: In the Mail Setup - Outlook window, click Show Profiles, select your profile, and click Properties. Then click Email Accounts.
Step 3: In the Account Settings window, click New to add a new email account.
Step 4: Select Manual setup or additional server types and click Next.
Step 5: Choose POP or IMAP and click Next.
Step 6: Fill in the following details:
  • Your Name: The name that will appear in your emails.
  • Email Address: (e.g., username@yourdomain.com)
  • Account Type: IMAP (Recommended)
  • Incoming mail server: Check Site Tools > Email > Mail Configuration
  • Outgoing mail server (SMTP): Same as above
  • User Name: Full email address
  • Password: Your email password
Step 7: Click More Settings > Outgoing Server > Check "My outgoing server requires authentication".
Step 8: Under the Advanced tab:
  • Incoming server (IMAP): 993, SSL/TLS
  • Outgoing server (SMTP): 465, SSL/TLS
Step 9: Click OK > Next and test the account. Click Finish once done.
Note: Double-check settings in Site Tools > Email > Mail Configuration tab.

Edit Existing Email Account Settings in Outlook

If you can't find a way to edit your current settings, try this workaround:

Alternative: Remove the account and re-add it:
  • File > Account Settings > Account Settings
  • Select the account and click Remove
  • Follow the setup steps again to re-add the account
Note: Always save your email settings before making changes.
